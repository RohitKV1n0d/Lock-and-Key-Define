Rules And Guidelines

  • Lock and key is an individual event comprising 10 rounds.
  • The platform will be our own website
  • Contestants are requested to use a PC or Laptop
  • It is advised to have an uninterrupted internet connection.
  • The event will last for 24 hours and results will be published afterwards.
  • The participation will be on a first come first serve basis.
  • Your hunt will be only completed if and only if you complete all the rounds.
  • Hints System
    • Participants will be given 5 hints each at the Start of the Hunt.
    • Each round has 3 hints to Choose from.
    • Once you open an hint, You'll only be able to open the next one in 2 hours
    • Each of the 3 hints in a round has a penalty time set for them respectively
      • Hint 1 ----- 1 hour
      • Hint 2 ----- 2 hour
      • Hint 3 ----- 3 hour
    • The penalty time will be added when the Participant has finished the hunt.
  • The winners will be decided by the top Participants with least time taken to complete the hunt with the added penalties
  • Total time taken for all Participants is considered from start of the HUNT(5:40 pm) to the time of completion with the added penalties.
  • The starting time of each Participant is common, July 8th 5:40pm.
